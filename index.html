    How to earn credits

    Gain XP while the RuneMetrics XP tracker is visible. Every 10 XP awards 1 credit, rounded up.

    Each XP drop also has a random chance to award a free pack. Free packs are more likely from higher XP drops.

    You can also sell cards from your collection. Extra copies can be sold together with the Sell duplicates button.

    Credits are used to buy card packs.
